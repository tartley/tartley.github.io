Trip Report: The Chelsea Arts Club

Jonathan Hartley

2007-03-14 10:23

Comments

Source

Dinner last night spent under the beady eye of painter James McNeill Whistler, founder of the divine Chelsea Arts Club, courtesy of Nicholas Furness and his Mum. Entertainment provided by the redoubtable mad Greek artist Renos, gadget advice and cheap tech hand-me-downs from Greg, and a new massage therapist, Elena, picked up along the way. A highly profitable evening, methinks.
