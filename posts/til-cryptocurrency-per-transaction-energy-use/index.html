    TIL: Cryptocurrency per transaction energy use

    Jonathan Hartley

    2021-05-13 16:09

    Comments

    For my own calibration, approximate current energy use for a single transaction in various cryptocurrencies:

    Currency Symbol Energy use
    per txn(kWh)    		 Equivalent to
    Bitcoin BTC 710 Hot tub, 120 hours
    Ethereum ETH 62 Nissan Leaf, 1.5 charges
    Litecoin LTC 19 Clothes washer, 8 loads
    Chia XCH 2 Dishwasher, 1 hour
    Cardano ADA 0.5 Computer & monitor, 1 hour
    Dogecoin DOGE 0.12 Humidifier, 1 hour
    Ripple XRP 0.0079 Amazon echo telling 2 jokes

    Sources:

    • I got most crypto energy use from this roundup.
    • For Chia, I did my own hacky calculation. Chia Power estimates 6MW in total is used by the Chia network, at current size of 4EiB. Meanwhile, Chia Explorer shows a hard-to-read graph which I'm eyeballing to get a rough average of 3,000 transactions per hour. 6 Megawatts divided by 3k per hour gives an energy use per transaction of 2kW.
